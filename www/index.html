Typeface

Inercia is a sans serif, display typeface designed by Gilmar Lira.

It is intended to be used in extremely large sizes, such as outdoor advertising, store signs, and title heads.

Process

The original inspiration came from Opera Rock logo, a brazillian clothing brand. Although the logo has been redesigned since then, you can still see a few connections, particularly in the curve of the red outline.
